A standard kindergarten curriculum focuses on the overall development of young children through fun and engaging activities. It includes basic learning in English, math, and general knowledge. Children learn the alphabet, numbers, colors, shapes, and days of the week. They also develop social skills, good manners, and healthy habits. Storytelling, drawing, singing, dancing, and games help in physical and emotional growth. Activities like cutting, pasting, and coloring improve motor skills. The curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation for future learning in a playful and caring environment.
